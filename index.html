What to Expect -

Here we have a mock up of our tool. If you would like to read more about the motivation, user study or future directions, please check out the tabs above! At the top you can enter the semantic scholar URL to any paper of interest. You will then get a drop down hierarchy of all the paper's references grouped by topic, along with a suggestion for 3-4 predicted foundational background papers. Below you will see a few summary plots that allow you to explore which references from the paper may be most relevant to you.

How to Use:

Enter any Semantic Scholar URL in the box below

What to Expect -

Once you click submit for your paper of interest, you will see a drop down hierarchy of all the paper's references grouped by topic. You can click on the arrows to see the individual papers.

To get a few recommendations and visually explore the references, select your topics of interest by selecting the checkboxes in the hierarchy. Then click, the show graph button. (This will take a few seconds)
